TASK 1 TEST CASES
=================

T1-01: Minimum three threads
Purpose:
Confirm that three POSIX worker threads are created and terminated.

Expected result:
All three threads complete their work and the final job count is 9.

Actual result:
Three worker threads completed a total of 9 jobs.

Status:
PASS


T1-02: Shared resource under load
Purpose:
Confirm that the completedJobs shared variable is protected correctly.

Expected result:
No updates are lost and the final result remains 9.

Actual result:
Expected completed jobs: 9
Actual completed jobs: 9

Status:
PASS


T1-03: Valid round-robin scheduling
Input:
Processes: 3
Burst times: 5, 4, 2
Time quantum: 2

Expected result:
Processes execute cyclically until all remaining times reach zero.

Actual result:
P1 completion: 11, waiting: 6, turnaround: 11
P2 completion: 10, waiting: 6, turnaround: 10
P3 completion: 6, waiting: 4, turnaround: 6
Average waiting time: 5.33
Average turnaround time: 9.00

Status:
PASS


T1-04: Invalid time quantum
Input:
Processes: 3
Burst times: 5, 4, 2
Time quantum: 0

Expected result:
The invalid time quantum is rejected safely.

Actual result:
Error: The value must be greater than zero.

Status:
PASS


T1-05: Repeated stability test
Purpose:
Run the application three times to check for deadlock, hanging or
incorrect shared-resource results.

Expected result:
Every execution terminates successfully and produces 9 completed jobs.

Actual result:
All three executions terminated successfully.
Each execution produced 9 completed jobs.

Status:
PASS
